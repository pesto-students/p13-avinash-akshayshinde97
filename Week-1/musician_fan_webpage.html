Calvin Harris

Calvin Harris, born Adam Richard Wiles on January 17, 1984, is a renowned Scottish DJ, record producer, singer, and songwriter. He gained international fame for his electronic and dance music tracks and has consistently topped the charts with his infectious beats and innovative soundscapes. His contributions to the music industry have earned him numerous awards and accolades, making him one of the most influential figures in contemporary music.
